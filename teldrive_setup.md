# TelDrive Setup Guide

I have installed the necessary tools. To complete the setup, please provide your Telegram API credentials.

## Step 1: Get Credentials
1. Go to [my.telegram.org](https://my.telegram.org) and get your **API ID** and **API Hash**.
2. Message [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather) on Telegram to create a new bot and get the **Bot Token**.

## Step 2: Update Configuration
I have created a template at `/root/.teldrive/config.toml`. You can also add these as environment variables in [Zo Secrets](/?t=settings&s=advanced).

## Step 3: Start TelDrive
Once configured, I will run TelDrive as a managed service on your Zo.
